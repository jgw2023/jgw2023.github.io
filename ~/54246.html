Pertti Paasio - Wikipedia Pertti Paasio. Pertti Kullervo Paasio (born 2 April 1939 in Helsinki) is a Finnish politician from the Social Democratic Party. Paasio participated in the municipal politics of Turku and was elected into the municipal council in 1965. Paasio became member of the Parliament in July 1975, however, he lost the seat in the 1979 parliamentary elections.
