Sunpro Alwin - Wikipedia The Sunpro Alwin (サンプロ・アルウィン) is a multi-use stadium in Matsumoto, Japan.It is currently used mostly for football matches and is the home ground of Matsumoto Yamaga FC.The stadium has a capacity of 20,396 spectators. Its nickname, Alwin, is a blend of the words "Alps" (as in the Japanese Alps) and "wind".. It was formerly known as Matsumotodaira Park Stadium.
